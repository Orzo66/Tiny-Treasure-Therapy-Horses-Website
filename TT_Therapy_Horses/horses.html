Meet Our Therapy Horses

Fabio

Fabio, born in 1999, is a gentle and patient black miniature horse gelding.

He is very skilled in working with individuals who have memory issues and disabilities, both physical and intellectual.

Justice

Justice is a sorrel and white pinto minature horse gelding born in 2021.

He is the clown in our herd, always making us laugh.

He is bold and brave and willing to go anywhere we ask him to go.

His silly personality and stunning good looks make him a favorite of many.

Tilly

Tilly is a bay roan minature horse mare and the only girl in our herd.

She is the youngest, born in 2023.

She is kind and quiet and loves attention and petting.

Fun fact about Tilly: she turns dark brown when her winter coat comes in, so it's like owning two totally different horses from spring to winter.
