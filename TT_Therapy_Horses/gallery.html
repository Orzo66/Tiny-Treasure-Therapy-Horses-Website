All of our therapy horses undergo regular training here on our farm to simulate the experiences they will have once they enter a care facility.

They are proficient with wheelchairs, walkers, canes, and scooters. The bond they have with Heather is key to allowing the horses to peacefully engage with our clients and bring comfort and calm.
